# HR Insights & Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Overview
This project presents an interactive HR analytics dashboard built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition patterns, demographic distribution, and workforce trends. The dashboard enables structured evaluation of turnover drivers and workforce composition.

## Key Metrics
- **Total Employees:** 1416  
- **Total Attrition:** 229  
- **Attrition Rate:** 16.2%  
- **Average Age:** 37  
- **Average Salary:** 6.5K  
- **Average Years at Company:** 7.0  

## Dashboard Capabilities
- Attrition analysis by gender and job role  
- Education field distribution analysis  
- Age group-based attrition breakdown  
- Salary slab-wise attrition segmentation  
- Years of service attrition trend tracking  
- Department-level demographic comparison  

## Key Insights
- Overall attrition rate stands at **16.2%**, indicating moderate workforce turnover.  
- Higher attrition observed in specific job roles and lower salary slabs.  
- Mid-career age groups contribute significantly to total attrition.  
- Attrition trends vary across years of service, highlighting early-tenure exits.
